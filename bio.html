Biography

DABEERUDDIN SYED (Member, IEEE) is a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECEN) at Texas A & M University (TAMU), College Station, U.S.A. Mr. Syed received his M.Sc. degree in Data Science and Engineering from Hamad bin Khalifa University, Qatar in 2018 and Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) from University College of Engineering, Osmania University, India in 2013. He has industrial work experience of three years. He started his career as a Database Administrator from Oracle Pvt. Ltd. He then attained experience and skills as a Research Assistant, and as Teaching Assistant on multiple occasions. He has previously assisted in teaching courses such as Electrical Circuits, Learning from Data, and Introduction to Data Science with Python Programming. His primary research areas include smart grids, big data analytics, load forecasting, and distributed computing. He has published over 18 research articles and has one book chapter to his credit.
